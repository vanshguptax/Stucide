According to World Health Organisation ,

Approximately 800,000 people die by suicide every year,
which translates to about 66,667 suicides per month,
15,385 per week , 2,191 per day,
and nearly 1 to 2 suicides every minute globally.

By the time you read this, one person will have died by suicide somewhere in the world, highlighting the urgent need for mental health support and awareness.

Psychology Behind suicides

Suicide can be influenced by several psychological factors. People who are dealing with mental health issues like depression often feel hopeless and overwhelmed. They might think that their problems will never get better and believe they are a burden to others. Feeling very lonely, having experienced past trauma, or facing a lot of stress can also increase the risk. Sometimes, a lack of support and coping skills makes people feel like suicide is the only way to escape their pain. Understanding these feelings is important for helping prevent suicide and providing support.

STORIES OF STRENGTH

This section is dedicated to survivors who have overcome suicidal thoughts or attempts, and those who have lost loved ones to suicide. Here, we share stories of strength, healing, and hope to inspire others and remind everyone that they are not alone.

Eminem

RAPPER

Before the release of his single "The Real Slim Shady", Eminem attempted to take his life with an overdose of Tylenol. He was distraught over his then girlfriend Kim leaving him and preventing him from seeing his daughter.

Owen Wilson

ACTOR

In August 2007, 'Loki' star Owen Wilson was found with his wrists slit in his Santa Monica home by his brother. The actor was rushed to the hospital and given the necessary treatment.

Michael Jackson

MUSICIAN

In June 2005, a bogus email reporting on a suicide attempt by the "king of pop" (just before he was found not guilty) infected computers worldwide. some sources claim Jackson did try to off himself that December.

Mike Tyson

BOXER

In September 1988, the then-undisputed heavyweight champion crashed his car into a tree in what the New York Daily News described as a suicide attempt.

J.K. Rowling

WRITER

Rowling has been vocal about her struggles with depression and suicidal thoughts also seeking help played a crucial role in her recovery. Before her success as an author.

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson

ACTOR

Johnson has shared his experiences with depression and overcoming suicidal thoughts after his mother attempted suicide when he was young.

Words Left Behind

Every life touched by suicide leaves behind a ripple of untold stories, heartache, and unanswered questions. In this space, we honor those who have passed through the words of their loved ones. These reflections are shared with hope—hope that by listening, we may find understanding, connection, and a reminder that no one has to carry their pain alone.
